Methods

In this paper, we propose a novel action-conditioned policy pretraining paradigm, which captures important features in the neural representation relevant to the decision-making and benefits various downstream tasks.

The method consists the following steps:

  1. We first collect a large corpus of driving videos with a wide range of weather conditions, from wet to sunny, from all across the world.
  2. We then train a inverse dynamics model with a small amount of labeled data and use it to generate action pseudo labels for each collected frame.
  3. We finally develop a novel policy pretraining method called Action-conditioned COntrastive Learning (ACO) that incorporates the action information in the representation learning.
